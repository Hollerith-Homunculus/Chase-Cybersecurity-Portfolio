# Home Lab Risk Assessment

**Subfolder Focus:** Risk identification, assessment, and mitigation documentation for the home lab environment.

## Overview
This subfolder contains risk assessment artifacts for my cybersecurity home lab. It demonstrates practical application of risk management principles from my Master’s program and industry frameworks (NIST basics).

These documents show my ability to evaluate threats, document controls, and maintain a living risk register — skills highly valued in SOC Analyst, GRC, and MSP roles.
